About Me

IT Support Specialist and Creative Digital Editor

IT Support & Digital Services

I have over 7 years of hands-on experience in IT Support, including 3 years providing both remote and on-site technical support. My experience includes troubleshooting hardware and software issues, maintaining Windows systems, and helping keep business operations running smoothly through reliable technical support.

My technical experience includes working with Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Windows Support, Networking, VPNs, Help Desk operations, and Remote Desktop tools. I also have hands-on experience supporting specialized systems such as Hikvision CCTV, POS systems, SQL Server Management Studio, HRIS, RFID attendance systems, and ZKTeco devices.

Beyond IT support, I also provide creative digital services, including photo editing, video editing, and AI-powered photo restoration using Adobe Photoshop, Filmora, and CapCut Pro. I enjoy learning new technologies and continuously improving my technical and creative skills to deliver dependable IT support and high-quality digital content.
